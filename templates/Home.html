FAQ

Common questions, answered clearly.

Do customers need to install an app? +

No. Orders can be placed directly from the browser after scanning the QR code, and sellers also manage operations from the web.

How do payments work? +

The system supports UPI and cash workflows, and sellers can configure their preferred payment setup.

Can delivery charges be configured? +

Yes. Delivery rules can be handled according to your store settings, including flat fee and percentage-based models.
